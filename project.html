Overview

MyCarPal App

We want the Carpal app to be helping, helping you, your friends and family, by organising your regular commutes, while sharing the cost.

Imagine an app you log into where you can find other users who want to travel to the same destination as you - whether it is a regular commute into your local business centre for work or a one-off trip down to the coast to visit family or friends. MyCarPal App is a cost efficient, user friendly app which will be offered on both the Android and IOS operating systems.

There are a lot of ride sharing apps out there but none that are future thinking, brings people together to share the cost of their travel requirements, and reduces our environmental footprint on this planet.

Imagine MyCarPal App.

Motivation

We want the Carpool app to help people and the environment. To allow anyone to have the opportunity to organise regular stress-free, low fare commutes. Making things less complicated and allowing travellers to go further than metro areas. Putting travellers’ minds at ease with safety and security at the heart of its design while reducing your carbon footprint created by too many cars on the roads, it's a win-win for everyone. 

But why go with the MyCarPal app when there are so many other ride-sharing apps already on the market? Uber and Lyft could be considered more of a replacement for taxi services and are based around profit and price-fixing. Owning your own car can be expensive to run and maintain, not to mention the vehicle's original purchase price. Maybe you do not even need the use of a car more than once or twice a year. Think of the environmental footprint the use of a vehicle has.

Additionally, there's public transport. It is costly and not easy to use with its constantly changing and confusing timetables, and safety is always a problem. To top it off, public transport does not always go in the most straightforward route you are after.

We see many reasons why customers would choose our app over others and ultimately our app is designed to make a difference in what can be one of the most stressful parts of people’s lives.

Description

When the user first downloads My Carpool App, they must register their name, phone number, email, license details and payment details.  These will then be verified.  You can then choose your username.

The user’s privacy will be prioritised, you will only need to share the relevant information with other users, e.g., “Blue Nissan - clean" all other information will be kept private.

Once they have been verified, the user can scroll through local boards, looking for users travelling in the same direction. They will be able to filter to add your destination, for example, from Melbourne to Sydney.  

You can also make a post and flag the date and time they are travelling, for example, “A trip to Sydney from Melbourne on Monday the 5/4 leaving at 9 am and looking for two passengers”.

You post your trip and can wait for other people to reply. When they answer, you can accept the share and arrange a pick-up point. If you are a user looking for the same date and destination, you will receive a notification.

“Hi user Benny82 is looking to travel from Sydney to Melbourne on 5/4, leaving at 9 am in your local area. Would you like to accept the ride?”

Once the price has been successfully negotiated the app will take a flat .99c fee transaction from both uses, this will be the only charge in an effort to keep costs low for the user and make the app more competitive with its competitors.

The App can also have a hitchhiker function, that can use people's geolocation to send notifications to people in the area.

For example, a person can be driving from Melbourne to Sydney and receive a notification “Thumbs up going my way?”

If the driver opens the notification, they'll see the information from the person looking to hitchhike “user Benny82’s is hitchhiking to Canberra. They are looking for a ride he's willing to compensate $25.”    

During the ride, the App shows the driver's destination. If anyone feels unsafe at any time, they can be an emergency button, which they can press to report any abuse against the other users and even put them through to emergency services. Although we want people to have happy, safe trips, I feel it is essential to have an emergency feature. Once the ride has finished, the transaction will occur, and both driver and passenger go in separate directions. If it is a regular commute ride, both users can choose to set up a regular commute trip in the App

Tools

The technical requirements for the application do not seem to be so complicated and it could vary by the design of the app. Ideally, the MyCarPal application will be built as a mobile app because the user should be able to use it whenever or wherever they need to. Furthermore, mobile apps could use GPS to better obtain the geolocation data of the user that is necessary for operating the application. Nevertheless, web pages would still be very useful to introduce the application and to support customer services.

When the app is designed to be a mobile app, it should be able to run by both android and iOS operating system. It could either be done by creating separate apps for each platform or to use a development software to build an app that can run by both operation systems. Android app could be developed by using popular Android IDEs such as Android Studio and Gradle in Kotlin or Java language. iOS software could use XCode and Apple Developer Tools by primarily using Swift or C language.

Alternatively, there are many software developments kit that can produce an app that is compatible with both android and iOS. Flutter could be a good option to choose because it enables a cross-platform app development to add/remove features and test them with reduced development time.

Outcome

The Outcome will be an app that is a user-friendly ride-sharing app helping customers travel more efficiently that runs on both android and iOS operating systems. That lets you choose the way you want to travel and the direction you want to travel, with a competitive flat fee cost structure that puts more profit into the user's wallets and the added benefit of saving money. An app that prioritises its users' safety and security, respecting its user's data privacy, giving people peace of mind when every they use our app. You'll be able to interact with your local community in a way that public transport does not allow. And most importantly, you'll be able to reduce your carbon footprint.

Users will have security and peace of mind that their travel needs are being met. No more fights about who is driving, where are we going, what time will we get there? All these questions are sorted out before you go anywhere. Your upfront costs for travelling already sorted.
